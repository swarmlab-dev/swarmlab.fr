About

Lucien Loiseau

Lucien is a creative mind with a passion for using technology to connect people, devices and places. With over a decade of experience, he woven his innovative spirit into diverse projects. As Director of Network & Services at Nodle, he architected the first incentivized wireless IoT network, pioneering blockchain integration and fostering technical evolution. Lucien co-founded Coalition Network, shaping a privacy-first contact tracing app combating COVID-19. At RightMesh, he led mesh networking innovation contributing to global standards. His academic journey, including a Ph.D. in System and Telecommunication, reflects his dedication to cutting-edge research. Let's collaborate and bring your tech dreams to life!
